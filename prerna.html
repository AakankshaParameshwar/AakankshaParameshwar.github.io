Prerna - Your support link

Prerna is mobile application that provide a support network to reduce stress associated with caregiving. It's not just an app. It's a social change.

Project Details

Role: UX Designer
Duration: 5 months
Team: 3 members
Tools Used: Sketch, Adobe InDesign

Contribution

User research (Surveys/Interview)
Empathy Mapping
Wireframes

Overview

About 5 million people in the United States are living with Dementia. And, 15 million people are caregiving for them. Dementia Caregivers are found to provide 20-40 hours of unpaid care each week, which amounts to 18 Billion Work Hours lost every year! Not only are they missing a portion of their income, but they also spend more than $5,000 a year from their savings causing immense financial and emotional stress. 49% of Dementia caregivers are found to experience high levels of stress which leads to 63% of them experiencing signs of depression which also leaves their immune systems affected for the next three years.

Understanding the User

We conducted a survey where 37 respondents (caregivers) where asked a series of questions about their daily routine, challenges they faced while taking care of their loved ones.



Our surveys showed that caregivers were not asking for help and Friends and Family wanted to, but didn’t know how and when to help. Care providers echoed that caregivers had unmet needs, which the medical system did not consider a priority.

The following pain points resurfaced throughout our study.

  • Lack of Communication
  • Hesitation in reaching out for help
  • Lack of support and appreciation

Persona



The first thing you need to know about caregiving is that it takes a village to take care of a loved one

45 year old caregiver, Ann Arbor

As we met with more families, we heard Matt’s story being narrated over and over again. In most cases caregiving for dementia patients ended up being a thankless job And, even with family offering help, there seemed to be issues due to miscoordination and miscommunications.

Product Concept

Prerna is a mobile app service that enables primary dementia caregivers to effectively seek out help from their close network of friends and family without hesitation and receive the due appreciation for their effort. It also enables friends and family to offer help in real ways that relieves stress off of primary caregivers.



Caregivers can share stories with their network who can then act on these by either showing some love, offering help or by just acknowledging the effort. Apart from having their support system in one place, one of the key features of this service is the contextually aware shared calendar.



The service sends passive prompts, based on location and availability, to the network suggesting them to offer help. That way the caregivers do not feel the stigma of seeking support or the stress of coordinating care. Prerna does it for them. Apart from this the service also sends appropriately timed reminders to the network to check up on the primary caregivers or patients.



Most of these features exist in the market and they have been proven out by other competitors but none of them exist in one place, or designed specifically for dementia caregivers and honestly most of them are pretty dumb. It is the contextual intelligence and behavioural techniques built into Prerna that makes it different from any other productivity tool that's out there.
